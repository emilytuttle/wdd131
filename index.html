About Me

My name is Emily. I was born and raised in Utah in the United States. I work as a product photographer and shipping manager for a jeweler and as an airfare expert for a flight deals company. I like to play piano, do pyrography, and rock climb.

Pyrography

The word pyrography comes from Greek and means "writing with fire." I usually explain it as drawing with a soldering iron. I occasionally do commissions, but mostly do personal projects to relax and have fun. I'm not great at color theory, but this is an art form that works for me to just shade and add texture.
